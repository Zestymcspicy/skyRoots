Thunders

Nyew Jigwe'k

Welcome to the Thunders board game!

Thunder's board is made up of modular tiles that form together into "the Land" as the game is being played, then in the second half of the game, are taken apart one tile at a time until the Land is no more. This feature of waxing and waning (like the moon) is the first evidence of the life cycle theme of Thunders, drawn from the Nyew Jigwe'k or the four Directions, as known by my tribe, the Prairie Band Potawatomi. More information about the cultural aspects and inspiration for the Thunders game can be found in the Culture section elsewhere on this site.
